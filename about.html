About IRMDC


About the Indian Rare Movement Disorder Consortia

The Indian Rare Movement Disorder Consortia (IRDC) combines specialist physicians, scholars with an interest in sharing on current studies and advancing movement disorder diagnosis and treatment.

The goal of the IRDC is to share knowledge, interest among all notable people involved in clinical and research on movement disorders within India, to engage in activities and co-operation.
